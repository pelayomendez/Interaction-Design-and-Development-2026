CRUD is the foundation of every data-driven app. Here's the complete pattern with Supabase.

CREATE await supabase.from('items').insert({ name: 'new item' }) READ await supabase.from('items').select('*').eq('id', 1) UPDATE await supabase.from('items').update({ done: true }).eq('id', 1) DELETE await supabase.from('items').delete().eq('id', 1)
